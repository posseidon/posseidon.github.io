Grammarly - the digital writing tool

5 min read

Based on Wikipedia, tt's using AI and natural language processing to aid users to get better in writing both formal and informal.

My goal is to improve my terrible writing skills in English. While looking around on the internet for writing aid solutions, the best one is Grammarly. It has the following features:

  • checking my spelling and grammar at the same time
  • dictionary with synonyms
  • checking for clarity and correctness of my text
  • plagiarism check

and much more features that are available in the premium package.

It supports Microsoft Word and Outlook, Google Drive and Chrome, really simple to install and use. After using the free version on a daily basis for a week I can see that

  • I'm paying more attention to structuring my sentences.
  • Repeating the same grammatical mistakes over and over again until I learn them.
  • Not repeating myself.
  • Writings are getting better, clear and focused on the topic.

Considering Premium package

I'm thinking about paying for the premium package, however, it's quite expensive. The monthly fee is 29.95 USD/month. If you choose the quarterly plan then it's 19.38 USD/month. The best value for money option is to choose the annual plan then it's 11.66 USD/month. It'sIt's obvious that obvious Grammarly is persuading us to select the annual package. I will update this post and share further experiences if I have chosen the premium package.
